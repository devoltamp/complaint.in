The idea behind complaints.in

>> Suppose I have a complaint about something that I would like to get fixed but it's related to government or any other authorization.

It takes a lot of time to have some changes in the govt. processes. So, complaint.com will just speed up the process a lot (a transparent process)




How does it work?

let's say you have a complaint about train frequency in your area, what you'll have to do is that just upload some proof & describe your complaint. & not just that it'll also have something called the upvote and the down-vote & the post will go public, according to the location people can give upvote if the complaint is valid and a down-vote it it's not valid. And accordingly the government and the authorization can do their part of the job. (Image)
